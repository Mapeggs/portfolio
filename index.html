Projects

DodgeBall

It's a local multiplayer game where you and your friend each control either Player 1 or Player 2 on the same keyboard. The goal is to throw balls at each other, and the first player to score 3 points wins.

It was created using JavaScript and Phaser 3, utilizing some free assets from itch.io and free audio soundtracks from Pixabay.

House Tour Trailer

I created a house touring trailer in Unity, using a camera configuration to move throughout the house. I also implemented special contraptions that trigger at specific times during the scene, showcasing special VFX like fire and a rigged door animation.

It was created using Unity, utilizing free assets from the unity store and I managed to use timeline managers to trigger specific events, along with particle systems, to create a more game-like experience.
